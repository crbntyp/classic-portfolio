Tried. Trusted. Taste.

Welcome to Pure Roast Coffee, the home of coffee roasting excellence. We’re truly passionate about the kind of coffee we produce. We follow rigorous processes, invest in the very best machinery and we only ever use the finest quality beans to produce the rich, fresh taste of pure quality coffee.

Innovation and Dedication

Always striving to improve your coffee, our beans are roasted using a state of the art Loring Convection Roaster. By convection roasting, hot air gently roasts the entire bean, retaining all the flavour and ensuring an even roast. This process allows for the ultimate roast, and therefore a superior taste. What’s more, it’s completely environmentally friendly. We travel far and wide to find the very best coffee beans so it’s only fitting that we use the world’s best Convection Roaster to process them.

Quality in Every Cup

We only source the best quality beans from over 50 countries around the world. Be it Africa, Central America, Asia…. we use beans from a single origin or a rich blend of different beans from various origins, using our skill and expertise to create the best-tasting coffee for you. Even the experts agree awarding us multiple Great Taste Awards over the past few years.

From Bean to Brew

Roasted and freshly ground we take an enormous amount of pride in our finest filter coffee. We have the expertise to create a distinctive blend be it medium, strong or ultra-strong in depth of flavour. When you open that first bag you’ll be met with the most delicious aroma of the freshest possible coffee.

How do you like yours?

We’ve embraced this cleaner quicker and more convenient method of delivering coffee to the consumer, ensuring a rich tasty blend with flavour literally vacuum packed in. The perfect accompaniment to your Senseo® Coffee Maker! These quick and easy pods come filled with fresh roasted coffee, which has been gently cooled then immediately packed to ensure none of the flavour escapes. So you are guaranteed the rich, fresh taste of pure coffee with every cup.
